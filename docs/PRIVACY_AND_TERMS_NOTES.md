# Privacy and Terms Implementation Notes

This repository does not substitute for legal review. Before public launch, publish documents appropriate to the jurisdictions, industries, channels, and data types involved.

At minimum disclose:

- The service uses automated and AI-assisted communication.
- What conversation, contact, appointment, call, and usage data is collected.
- The purposes, providers, retention periods, and deletion process.
- Whether calls are recorded; do not state that calls are recorded unless recording is actually enabled.
- How customers opt out of SMS and marketing communication.
- That AI responses may be inaccurate and urgent matters should use appropriate emergency channels.
- Restrictions against submitting passwords, payment-card data, Social Security numbers, medical records, or unlawful content.
- Subscription fees, included usage, overages, cancellation, refunds, support scope, and service availability.
- The roles of the agency, local business, hosting provider, model provider, telephony provider, CRM, and payment processor.

Configure the product and scripts to match the published policy. A disclosure that conflicts with actual behavior creates risk rather than reducing it.
