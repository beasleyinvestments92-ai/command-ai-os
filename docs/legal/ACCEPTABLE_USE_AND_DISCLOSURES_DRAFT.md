## Acceptable use

Customers must use COMMAND AI OS lawfully, transparently, and with appropriate human oversight. The service may not be used for deceptive impersonation, unlawful telemarketing, harassment, discrimination, fraud, credential collection, emergency dispatch without qualified oversight, regulated professional advice without licensed review, or collection of unnecessary sensitive data.

Customers must maintain documented consent where required for calls, recordings, SMS, email, automated messages, and AI processing. Opt-outs must be honored promptly. Contact lists may not be purchased or scraped in violation of law or provider policy.

## AI disclosure

Recommended customer-facing disclosure:

> You are communicating with an AI-assisted service for [BUSINESS NAME]. It can answer approved business questions, collect information, and help with scheduling. It may make mistakes. Ask for a human at any time.

## Call disclosure

Recommended opening disclosure where lawful:

The customer must adapt this language to applicable federal, state, and local consent requirements before activation.
